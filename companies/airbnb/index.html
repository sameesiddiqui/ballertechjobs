AirBnB Hiring process

Cultural fit is extremely important for AirBnB. During the non-technical interviews, your personality and culture fit for the company will be evaluated. AirBnB wants to ensure your values are in line with theirs, as they give employees significant freedom to work.

Be prepared to answer questions about your background, how you work in a team, and other questions about your work habits.

Like many other tech companies, there will be multiple coding interviews to test your problem solving abilities. Initially, there will likely be 1-2 phone screens before an invitation for an onsite interview. Once passing these, the questions will likely be 30-45 minute programming problems that will be done on a white board.
